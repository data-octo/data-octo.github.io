    Introduction: AI-Powered Job Hunting

    Before diving into tools and tactics, it's important to understand how AI is transforming the job search landscape. This book will guide you through leveraging powerful, free AI tools to gain a competitive edge in your job hunt. Whether you're a new graduate, an experienced professional, or a student seeking part-time work, AI can help you navigate the modern job market more effectively.

    Tool Categories & Examples

    • Language Models: ChatGPT, Gemini, Copilot, Claude (for brainstorming, writing, interview practice)
    • App Download Information: Many of these are web-based, but some like ChatGPT and Gemini have mobile apps available on iOS and Android app stores.
    • Resume Tools: Kickresume, Resume.com, Canva (for formatting, keyword suggestions)
    • App Download Information: Canva has mobile and desktop apps. Kickresume and Resume.com are primarily web-based.
    • Job Search: LinkedIn, Indeed, Google Jobs (for AI-powered job matching)
    • App Download Information: LinkedIn and Indeed have robust mobile apps for iOS and Android. Google Jobs is integrated into Google Search.
    • Writing Help: Grammarly, Quillbot (for error-free, professional writing)
    • App Download Information: Grammarly offers browser extensions, desktop apps, and mobile keyboards. Quillbot is primarily web-based.
    • Learning: Coursera, edX, YouTube (for upskilling, many with AI recommendations)
    • App Download Information: Coursera, edX, and YouTube all have mobile apps for iOS and Android.
    • Interview Prep: Pramp, InterviewBuddy, AI mock interview tools
    • App Download Information: Availability varies; some are web-based, while others might offer dedicated apps. Check their respective websites for details.
