# UBC Dreams: Five Students' Journey in the AI Era (2026–2030)

## Novel Overview

This is a story about five Chinese-descended students studying at UBC during the AI era (2026–2030). Agentic AI, physical AI, neural interfaces, and quantum computing are part of everyday life. From their freshman year in the Faculty of Science, they navigate major changes: switching majors, testing friendships, first loves, and searching for meaning. Each follows a different yet meaningful path.

## Features

- Future-tech setting: agentic AI, physical AI, neural interfaces, quantum computing
- Deep character portrayals: backgrounds, personalities, growth arcs
- Rich emotional threads: layered friendships and romances
- Reflections on meaning: tech and humanity explored philosophically
- Practical guidance: integrated information about UBC resources, government support, and career planning

## Main Characters

- Li Mingxuan — Aspiring entrepreneur (Canadian Chinese). Confident, tall, business-minded; seeks to found a socially responsible agentic AI company.
- Wang Xiaojie — Tech devotee (from Beijing). Focused, brilliant in algorithms, driven to build ethical agentic AI.
- Chen Siyu — Insightful and warm (Canadian Chinese). Biomedical-minded, aims to apply AI to medical diagnosis.
- Liu Xiaomei — Resilient organizer (from Shandong). Strong coordination skills, becomes a bridge between tech and people.
- Zhang Haoran — Human-centered leader (Canadian Chinese). Natural leader, focuses on ethics and human-centered AI.

## Themes

- Technology & humanity: how agentic AI and neural interfaces reshape education, healthcare, and daily life
- Deep interpersonal exploration: love, friendship, family expectations, and cultural interactions
- Practical value: UBC resources, Canadian policies, and career strategies for 2026–2030
- Meaning of life: balancing personal mission and social responsibility in a rapidly changing era
