# Chapter 5 — Job Preparation and Career Skills

## Senior Year Job Anxiety

By senior year, career pressure weighs on Wang Xiaojie. The market is competitive, and he needs a focused plan.

## Job Search Strategy

They tier target employers (dream companies, realistic options, safety choices), map a timeline (resume prep, campus recruiting, interview peak), do a skills gap analysis, and plan project work and certifications.

## Interview Preparation

Daily LeetCode practice, systems design study, open-source contributions, mock interviews, career-center coaching, and interview simulations help Xiaojie prepare. Resume optimization includes quantifying impact and highlighting projects.

## Co-op Value and Offers

Co-op experience proves decisive in interviews. Xiaojie leverages past internships and contributions, eventually choosing a Vancouver-based high-growth company that balances learning and work-life balance.

## Immigration and Long-Term Planning

They review PGWP (post-graduation work permits), provincial nominee streams (BC PNP Tech), Express Entry, and legal guidance for immigration strategy.

## Workplace Onboarding and Growth

Xiaojie adapts in the first weeks, focuses on learning company tech stacks, collaboration, and building a career plan: technical depth, leadership opportunities, and lifelong learning.
