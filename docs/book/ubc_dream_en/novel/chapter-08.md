# Chapter 8 — Financial Management: Investing in the Future

## Financial Awareness

As graduation approaches, Zhang Haoran raises financial planning as a key life skill. The group learns about Canadian banking, credit, taxes, budgeting, and investment basics.

## Budgeting and Saving

They adopt a budget model, build emergency funds, automate savings, and prioritize debt management. The 50/30/20 rule guides their short-term planning.

## Investment Fundamentals

They learn about compound interest, diversification, stocks, bonds, ETFs, TFSA and RRSP accounts, and long-term strategy. Each character designs a plan suited to their career stage.

## Risk Management and Insurance

They review insurance options, debt strategies, and financial health checks. They commit to continuous learning and periodic reviews of their plans.

## Financial Goals

Short-term: emergency fund, small investments
Mid-term: down payment, career growth
Long-term: financial independence, retirement planning, family support, philanthropy
