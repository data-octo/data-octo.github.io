# Chapter 6 — Academic Advancement: The Research Path

## Doctoral Ambitions

In junior year Chen Siyu discovers a passion for deep learning in medical imaging. Encouraged by a professor, she considers PhD study to pursue impactful research.

## PhD Application Preparation

She studies program types, application requirements (strong GPA, research experience, letters of recommendation, research statement), and target schools. She gains undergraduate research experience, internships, conference exposure, and publications.

## Selecting Advisors and Funding

She carefully contacts prospective advisors, prepares tailored research statements, applies for scholarships (NSERC, CIHR, Vanier), and weighs offers from top programs. She chooses a fully funded offer at a leading Canadian university for its advisor fit and resources.

## PhD Life and Career Options

She plans coursework, research milestones, conference participation, and eventual career outcomes: academia, industry research, or research-based startups. She prepares for the long but rewarding path ahead.
